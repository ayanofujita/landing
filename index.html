Key Features

Flora & Fauna

Its wildlife includes Philippine Brown Deer. One can also find several orchid species and other rare flora such as the pitcher plant.

Body of Water

Falls, rivers and small lakes mark the area. The mountain provide water necessities for irrigation, hydroelectric power production and aquaculture.

Swim in The Clouds

At lower elevations, the mountainside has a mossy forest veiled with fog, and at the peak, you're greeted by a sea of clouds.
